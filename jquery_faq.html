About Some of Our Parks

Lassen

  • Declared a park on August 9, 1916
  • Area around Lassen Peak is still ative with boiling mud pots and churning hot springs
  • 5 mile hike round trip
  • cinder cone volcano

Mammoth

  • "slow time" meaning Central Time
  • Cave tours offered
  • Park hikes and ride horses on more than 70 miles of trails.
  • Mammoth Cave Hotel available for lodging

Niagra

  • Named from French explorers who named the Indian tribe the "Neutrals".
  • Erodes at a rate of .7 meters per year
  • 15 people have challenged the falls of Niagra since 1901
  • Dog friendly
